## Rating Definitions

| Dimension | :green_circle: Green | :yellow_circle: Amber | :red_circle: Red |
|-----------|------|-------|-----|
| **Delivering Value** | We deliver great stuff regularly and stakeholders love it | We deliver but not always the most valuable things | We rarely deliver, or stakeholders don't see value |
| **Speed** | We get things done fast, no waiting | Decent pace but bottlenecks slow us | Too slow, constant waiting and blockers |
| **Tech Quality** | Code is clean, tests are solid, proud of our work | Some tech debt but manageable | Tech debt is crippling us |
| **Learning** | We learn new things constantly and share knowledge | Some learning happens | Stagnant, doing the same things the same way |
| **Mission** | We know exactly why our work matters and it excites us | We sort of know our mission | No idea why we're doing what we're doing |
| **Fun** | Love coming to work, enjoy what we do | It's OK, could be better | Dreading work |
| **Teamwork** | Great collaboration, trust, psychological safety | We work together but sometimes siloed | Dysfunctional, blame culture |
| **Support** | We get help from the org when we need it | Sometimes supported, sometimes not | We're on our own, no one helps |
